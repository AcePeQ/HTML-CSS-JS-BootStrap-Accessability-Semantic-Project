Check out our best friends

Check out who we're currently hosting in our garden full of love and happiness. Each dog is assigned to one of our team members, who teaches them, plays with them, and provides them with experiences, while also preparing them for the next step in their journey through life.

Max

Labrador weighing 30 kg. He is friendly and loyal, always eager to play and bond with people. Max loves outdoor activities and is very energetic, making him a perfect companion for active owners.

Bella

Beagle weighing 12 kg. She’s a curious and intelligent dog with a playful personality. Bella loves exploring new places and is always up for an adventure, though she can be a bit stubborn at times.

Rocky

Rottweiler weighing 40 kg. Known for his bravery and strong build, Rocky is also very protective of his family. He’s calm and collected at home but remains alert and ready to defend when needed.

Luna

Golden Retriever weighing 28 kg. She’s gentle, loving, and incredibly patient, making her great with children and other pets. Luna is a social dog who loves being around people and is always ready for cuddles.

Daisy

Dalmatian weighing 25 kg. Active and full of energy, she loves running and playing games, especially fetch. Daisy is also sociable, making her an ideal pet for families who enjoy outdoor activities.

Charlie

Poodle weighing 10 kg. He is highly intelligent and easy to train, with a playful and friendly demeanor. Charlie enjoys being the center of attention and loves showing off his tricks to everyone around him.
